# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes pizza sales data to uncover key business insights related to revenue, order patterns, product performance, and customer preferences.

The dashboard was built using **Microsoft Power BI** to enable interactive exploration of sales trends across time, category, and product dimensions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The primary objective of this analysis is to:

* Identify top-performing and underperforming pizza categories
* Analyze sales trends across days, months, and time periods
* Understand customer ordering behavior
* Provide actionable insights to improve revenue and operations

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

The dashboard tracks the following business-critical metrics:

* **Total Orders:** 21,350
* **Total Revenue:** ₹817,860.05
* **Total Pizzas Sold:** 49,574
* **Average Order Value:** ₹38.31
* **Average Pizzas per Order:** 2.32

---

## 📈 Insights & Findings

### 🗓️ Time-Based Insights

* Orders peak on **weekends (Friday & Saturday evenings)**, indicating strong demand during leisure hours.
* **May and July** show the highest order volumes, suggesting seasonal or promotional impact.
* Daily order trend shows consistent activity with a peak towards the end of the week.

👉 **Business Implication:**
Increase staffing, inventory, and delivery capacity during weekends and peak months.

---

### 🍕 Category Performance

* **Classic category** contributes the highest number of orders.
* Revenue contribution is fairly distributed across categories, with no extreme dominance.

👉 **Business Implication:**
Classic pizzas act as volume drivers — ideal for combo deals and upselling.

---

### 📏 Size-Based Insights

* **Large size pizzas generate the highest revenue share (~45%)**
* Smaller sizes contribute significantly less to revenue

👉 **Business Implication:**
Focus on pricing strategy and bundling around large-size pizzas to maximize revenue.

---

### 🏆 Product-Level Performance

#### Top Performers

* **Thai Chicken Pizza** generates the highest revenue
* **Classic Deluxe Pizza** leads in both quantity sold and number of orders

#### Underperformers

* **Brie Carre Pizza** shows lowest revenue, quantity, and orders

👉 **Business Implication:**

* Promote top performers aggressively
* Re-evaluate or remove underperforming items from the menu
